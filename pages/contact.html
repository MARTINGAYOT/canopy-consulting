Common Questions

Before you reach out.

Is the discovery call really free?
Yes, completely. It is a 30-minute call to understand your business and see if we are the right fit. No pitch, no obligation, no invoice.
Do I need to be in Belize to work with you?
No. Canopy Consulting is remote-first. We deliver all services via video calls, shared documents, and cloud tools. We work with clients anywhere in the world.
What if I am not sure which phase I need?
That is exactly what the Business Audit is for. We assess where you are, identify what is missing, and tell you clearly what you need. Start there.
How long do engagements typically take?
Phase 1 and 2 packages typically take 3 to 6 weeks depending on scope. The Business Audit takes 1 to 2 weeks. Retainer engagements are ongoing monthly.
What does the Business Audit include?
A deep-dive call, a full review of your current operations, written findings, identification of inefficiencies and missed revenue, and a clear prioritized action plan.
Can I start with just one service and expand later?
Absolutely. Most clients start with a Business Audit, then move into Phase 1 or 2 work. The journey is designed to be taken one step at a time.
